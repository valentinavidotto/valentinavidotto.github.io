Current thesis

The rate cut cycle is being misread by equities.

Markets are pricing in a soft-landing scenario that assumes cuts arrive before growth materially slows. History suggests the opposite — the first cut has historically marked the beginning of risk-off, not a permission slip for further upside. I'm positioned cautiously in long-duration and selectively short cyclicals.
